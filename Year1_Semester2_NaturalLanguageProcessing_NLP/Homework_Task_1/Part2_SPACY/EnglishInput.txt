Fresh basil leaves are the main ingredient for pesto.
You must wash and dry the basil leaves carefully.
Pine nuts add a creamy texture to the sauce.
Garlic gives the pesto a strong and spicy flavor.
Extra virgin olive oil connects all the ingredients together.
Parmigiano Reggiano cheese adds a rich and salty taste.
Pecorino cheese makes the flavor even more intense.
A traditional marble mortar is best for crushing the ingredients.
You should crush the garlic and pine nuts first.
Add the oil slowly while mixing everything into a smooth paste.
